A video project for a multimedia project

Requirements

  • Choose a royalty free piece of music and incorporate 3 provided audio files into it
  • Edit the song using Audacity and apply sound effects to it
  • Apply image editing techniques using Photoshop to 3 photos
  • Incorporate edited photos into video
